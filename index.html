Microsoft Power Platform Fundamentals (PL-900)

Certification Overview

The Microsoft Power Platform Fundamentals certification is an entry-level credential designed for individuals seeking foundational knowledge of the Power Platform.

No prior experience or prerequisites are required.

Content Outline & Domain Weighting
